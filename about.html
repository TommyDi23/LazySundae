What we're About

Inspiration


Our passion for food and travel inspired us to bring the best flavours of Asia back into the west. On our travels we experienced the most wonderful flavours of exotic fruits and have recreated that taste to infuse them into our own desserts bringing the best of both cultures together. We only just the highest quality ingredients

Dedication


Theres nothing we enjoy more than seeing smiles on your faces. We enjoy blending and mixing ingredients to come up with new flavours. This isn't our job, its our hobby!

Bring your family
